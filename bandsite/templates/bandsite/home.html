Welcome to the Needtobreathe Fan Site!

NEEDTOBREATHE is an American rock band hailing from Seneca, South Carolina, known for their soulful and anthemic sound. Formed in 2000 by brothers Bear and Bo Rinehart, the band has evolved over the years to become a prominent force in contemporary rock music. Blending elements of Southern rock, folk, and alternative, NEEDTOBREATHE's music is characterized by powerful vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and dynamic instrumentals.

The band's name reflects their belief in the transformative and life-giving power of music. Their discography includes a diverse range of albums, showcasing their ability to explore various musical styles while maintaining a distinct, authentic sound. NEEDTOBREATHE has garnered acclaim for their energetic live performances, which often feature a mix of intimate ballads and high-energy rock anthems.

Beyond their musical achievements, the band members are recognized for their philanthropic efforts and commitment to making a positive impact. NEEDTOBREATHE continues to captivate audiences worldwide with their passionate storytelling and compelling musicality.
